Who We Are

ABOUT US

19+ Years of Excellence

GHUMMAN GROUP OF COMPANIES is one of the best mechanical engineering works in UAE. Our experience in welding, fabrication, erection, testing, flushing, commissioning, completion, maintenance, and repairing of critical mechanical components relating to various industries is quite rewarding when it comes to quality and services.

We work as a specified MEP contractor, design, build, construct, supply, install, service, upgrade, and maintain electro-mechanical systems & networks, utilities and equipment. Our facilities cover engineering, documentation, submittals approval, shop drawings, coordination drawings, commissioning, start-up, and as-built documentation.
